## The rounds (typical Staff loop, 2025–2026)
| Round | Who | ~Time | What they assess |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Recruiter screen | Recruiter | 30 min | Background, level fit, comp range, motivation |
| Hiring-manager | HM | 45–60 min | Role/team fit, leadership style, your narrative |
| Coding | Senior eng | 45–60 min | Pattern fluency, reasoning, communication |
| System design | Senior/Staff | 60–90 min | Depth, trade-offs, real-world constraints |
| Behavioral / managerial | Manager / Staff | 45–60 min | Scope, impact, influence, leadership |
| Peer / skip-level (optional) | Peer / skip | 30–45 min | Collaboration, culture, values |

Timeline: often ~5–6 weeks end to end at large companies.

## Why this matters
The recruiter and HM rounds set your *level* and *comp band* before you write a line of code.
Negotiation alone routinely moves total comp materially. Treat these as skilled rounds, not formalities.
