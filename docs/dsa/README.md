## Why this matters at Staff level (2026)
Senior/Staff coding rounds increasingly test **pattern recognition + trade-off articulation**
over raw LeetCode-hard volume — with AI writing boilerplate, your *reasoning* is the signal.
Aim to name the pattern in ~2 minutes and justify the approach, not just pass tests.
